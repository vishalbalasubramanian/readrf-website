About READ Research Foundation

The Responsible, Ethical AI Design (READ) Research Foundation is a globally oriented think tank committed to promoting ethical and explainable AI. Our mission is to advance trustworthy AI by combining deep engineering expertise with robust policy research.

What we study

  • Responsible and explainable AI design
  • Ethical considerations across AI, hardware, and systems engineering
  • Policy-relevant research for trustworthy AI deployment

Where we focus

  • Governance for edge AI, embedded systems, and infrastructure
  • Research applicable beyond large language models
  • Cross-disciplinary engagement with engineering and life sciences

Mission

To pioneer rigorous, interdisciplinary research and practical frameworks that ensure AI systems are designed and deployed with safety, accountability, and societal benefit in mind. We translate technical findings into actionable policy and engineering guidance.

Vision

A future where AI-driven systems are trustworthy by design, understood by stakeholders, and governed by evidence-based policy — across devices, infrastructure, and applications.

Our Approach

Interdisciplinary Research

Combine systems engineering, hardware expertise, ethics, and policy to deliver holistic insights.

Actionable Outputs

Whitepapers, toolkits, benchmarks, and governance guides targeted to engineers and policymakers.

Stakeholder Engagement

Co-design with industry, regulators, and affected communities to ensure practical uptake.
