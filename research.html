Our Research Focus

Responsible AI Design

Developing frameworks for AI systems that are ethical, explainable, and aligned with human values from the design stage.

AI-Hardware Integration

Investigating the ethical and practical implications of AI at the intersection of semiconductors, edge devices, and SoC architectures.

AI Policy & Governance

Creating research-driven policy recommendations and governance frameworks for responsible AI deployment in industry and society.

Explainable AI

Developing interpretable and accountable AI systems for technical, regulatory, and policy stakeholders.

Edge & Embedded AI

Studying AI applications in IoT, autonomous systems, and embedded devices with an emphasis on responsibility and trustworthiness.

Cross-Disciplinary Ethics

Examining AI applications across life sciences, engineering, and societal domains to promote holistic ethical frameworks.
