Shaping the Future of Responsible AI

Advancing ethical, explainable, and trustworthy AI at the intersection of engineering, policy, and society.

READ Research Foundation is a Canada-based think tank dedicated to pioneering research in responsible AI. We focus on bridging the gap between AI technology, engineering systems, and public policy to ensure AI is designed to be safe, accountable, and beneficial for society.
